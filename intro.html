Hi! I'm Jinyang and thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio page.

As a data engineer and machine learning specialist, I work with the latest technologies to translate data into valuable and comprehensible insights and predictions. My goal is to work in an agile environment and build end-to-end scalable machine learning applications that serve business goals, make the right decisions, save costs, and capitalizes on business opportunities

My experience includes data analysis, machine learning, big data, and cloud computer. I am an AWS cloud practitioner and I am certified in the Machine Learning Specialty. I am comfortable working in the cloud, using services such as EC2, S3, Sagemaker, lambda, dynamoDB, kineses (stream & firehose), cloudwatch, and more...

As someone passionate about AI, I am constantly keeping myself updated with industry conferences, skill training courses, informational webinars. In 2020, I attended Tableau's virtual conference. On January 8th I participated in the Women in Data Science Conference hosted by Standford University. In the same month on the 13th, I attended the virtual conference Unifying Data Pipelines and Machine Learning with Apache Spark & Amazon Sagemamker.

To keep my skills current, I also participate in Kaggle discussions and machine learning contests. In January 2021, I submitted my model for the Booking.com Data Challenge where I deployed a deep learning model to predict (and recommend) the final city of a list of destinations in a travel itinerary.

Data is my passion, my work, and my life. I am interested in building modular machine learning applications that can communicate with existing production systems. I want to use my skills to deploy a model to product and to produce actionable results that can have a positive impact.
